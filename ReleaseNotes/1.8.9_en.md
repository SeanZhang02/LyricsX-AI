# What's New

## Fixes

- **The preferences window no longer keeps popping up every few seconds** — When "Launch & Quit with Player" was enabled, the background helper crashed every time it tried to hand control back to the system, so macOS kept relaunching it, and every relaunch reopened the already-running app and pulled up the preferences window. The helper now exits cleanly and skips relaunching the app when it is already running.
